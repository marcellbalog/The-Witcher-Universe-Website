Welcome to the Witcher summary site

-----------------

This site contains the basics of the fantasy world of the Witcher. The universe was created by polish writer Andrzej Sapkowski in the 1990s. With recently peeking popularity, there are plenty of adaptations about the literary work in forms of games, comics and series. Mentioning series, a new Netflix adaptation is in the works at the moment, filming the scenes in Hungary, and starring Henry Cavill as the protagonist, Geralt.
